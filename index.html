FAQ

The questions every
operator asks us.

How is this different from HubSpot, Notion, or Zapier?
They store data or move it between tools. Klarios reads all of it and tells you what to do. HubSpot doesn't write your standup. Notion doesn't catch stuck deals. Zapier doesn't prioritise your day. Klarios does all three and ships the work.
Do I need to rip out my current tools?
No. Klarios connects to what you already have. HubSpot, Stripe, Notion, Meta, GA4, Linear, Slack, Intercom, Gmail, Calendly, Mixpanel, Xero, Mixpanel, GSC, and more. Nothing migrates.
Does the AI replace my team?
No. It replaces the part of their job they hate. Your Paid Media person still owns strategy. Klarios runs the daily report, flags underperforming ads, drafts the optimisation brief. Your person approves in 60 seconds and goes back to strategy.
How long does setup take?
10 minutes for the basic Home tile. 30 minutes to connect all your tools and upload documents. A week for your agents to learn your tone and brand. Then Monday writes itself.
What about my data?
Private workspace, encrypted at rest. Your data never trains a model. Every API call is scoped to your workspace. SOC 2 Type II in progress.
Who built this?
Operators. Klarios was built inside a real business to run a real business. Every feature shipped against the question "would we use this in our own daily standup?" If the answer was no, it didn't ship.
What if I already use Claude or ChatGPT?
Keep using them. Klarios isn't a chat interface, it's an operating layer. It pulls in your tools, makes decisions, runs agents, and ships work. Your chat subscription is for thinking. Klarios is for running.
Can I bring my own Anthropic key?
Yes, on every plan. If you want unlimited Claude inference, plug in your own API key and we only charge for the software. Heavy operators save $1-2k/month.

Built by a founder who needed this.

Klarios was born from running a real business and being tired of switching between 12 tools, chasing freelancers, and losing track of what matters.

I started YP Club in Dubai as a solo founder. Within six months I was the marketer, the sales team, the bookkeeper, the content creator, and the customer support line. My mornings looked the same every day: open Meta Ads Manager to check yesterday's spend, switch to Google Search Console to see if anything ranked, log into Xero to chase an overdue invoice, then flip to a Google Sheet that was pretending to be a CRM. By 10am I had touched eight tools and done zero real work.

I tried agencies. The SEO firm charged $3,000 a month and sent a PDF report I could have pulled from Search Console myself. The ads freelancer disappeared mid-campaign. The virtual assistant needed more managing than the tasks I gave them. What I actually needed was not another dashboard or another person to manage. I needed someone who could do the work. AI was getting good enough to reason through business problems, but nobody had connected it to the actual tools where work happens.

So I built Klarios. It is an AI agent platform where specialist agents connect directly to your business tools, read your real data, propose actions, and execute them when you approve. The SEO agent connects to Google Search Console and your website, finds keyword gaps, writes a blog post, and publishes it. The Paid Media agent creates Meta campaigns from a conversation. The Revenue agent tracks your pipeline and drafts follow-ups. They are not chatbots giving suggestions. They press the button.

The platform is live today. The SEO agent has published real blog posts to a production website. The CRM tracks leads from Calendly, Instantly, and manual entry. Content Studio generates images across seven AI models. Paid Media creates and manages campaigns via the Meta API. We are honest about the stage: some agents are further along than others, and we are building in public. What is next is deeper autonomy for Paid Media, Finance write tools through Xero, and Social Media scheduling to Instagram and LinkedIn.

Klarios is for founders running a real business who cannot afford to hire a marketing team, a sales rep, a bookkeeper, and a social media manager, but cannot afford to ignore those functions either. If you are the person doing everything yourself and know it is not sustainable, this is the platform we are building for you.

The endgame.

Every founder should have a team of specialists on day one. Not in three years when they can afford to hire. Not from an agency that charges $5,000 a month for a monthly report. From the moment they start.

Klarios is the operating system that makes that possible. Connect your tools, set your strategy, and your agents handle the rest. You stay in control. They do the work.

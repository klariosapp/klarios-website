Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

1. Who we are

Klarios ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at klarios.io and the platform at app.klarios.io. Klarios is an AI agent platform that connects to business tools and provides AI-powered specialists for marketing, sales, and operations.

For questions about this policy, contact us at hello@klarios.io.

2. What data we collect

Account data

When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and authentication credentials. Accounts are managed through NextAuth.

Workspace data

When you use Klarios, you create a workspace. Within your workspace, we store:

  • Chat messages between you and your AI agents
  • Tasks created by you or your agents
  • Documents you upload (strategy docs, brand guidelines, customer segments)
  • Auto-extracted lessons from agent conversations
  • Content generated through Content Studio (images, captions, posts)

Integration data

When you connect third-party tools (Meta Ads, Google Search Console, Xero, Calendly, Instantly, Instagram, LinkedIn, GitHub, Stripe, Mixpanel, Google Analytics), we store:

  • OAuth tokens or API keys required to access your accounts
  • Data pulled from those integrations (ad performance, keyword rankings, financial data, leads, analytics) as needed by your agents

Integration credentials are stored per-workspace and are never shared between workspaces.

Waitlist and contact data

If you join our waitlist, we collect your name, email address, WhatsApp number (optional), and role.

Usage data

We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the Klarios platform
  • To run your AI agents, including generating morning briefs, executing tasks, and providing recommendations
  • To connect to your third-party integrations and pull or push data on your behalf
  • To send you product updates, onboarding information, and support communications
  • To improve the platform based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns

4. AI processing

Klarios uses Anthropic's Claude API (Sonnet and Haiku models) to power agent reasoning. When you interact with an agent:

  • Your messages, relevant workspace context, and integration data are sent to Anthropic's API for processing
  • Anthropic's data usage policy applies to data processed through their API. Anthropic does not train on API inputs
  • Agent responses are stored in your workspace

5. Data retention

  • Chat history is compressed and archived nightly. Archives older than 20 days are automatically deleted
  • Account and workspace data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, your workspace data (messages, tasks, documents, integration credentials) will be deleted within 30 days
  • Waitlist data is retained until you request removal

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic -- for AI model processing via their API
  • Vercel -- our hosting provider
  • Third-party integrations you connect -- only the data required for the integration to function, and only when you initiate the connection

7. Data security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Database connections use SSL
  • Multi-tenant workspace isolation ensures no customer can access another customer's data
  • Integration credentials are stored encrypted in our database
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect any third-party integration at any time, which revokes our access to that service
  • Export your workspace data

To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@klarios.io.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

Finance Agent

P&L, invoices, runway, expenses. Monitored daily.

Connects to Xero and reads your financial data. Flags overdue invoices, forecasts runway, analyses expenses, and keeps you profitable without you opening Xero once.

Get Early Access

Capabilities

Complete financial visibility, automated.
P&L analysis -- reads your Xero profit and loss. Compares MTD to YTD trend. Flags unusual expense categories. Explains what changed and why.
Invoice chasing -- identifies overdue invoices, prioritises by amount and days overdue, drafts personalised chase emails. You approve and send.
Runway forecasting -- calculates months of runway from current bank balance, MRR, and average burn rate. Three scenarios: status quo, 20% MRR growth, 20% expense cut.
Expense monitoring -- watches for spikes in any expense category. Flags anything that looks unusual compared to your historical pattern.
Cash position -- live view of bank balance, MRR, MTD revenue vs MTD expenses, outstanding receivables.
Revenue tracking -- Stripe charges, subscriptions, MRR, and failed payments alongside Xero data. Full financial picture.

Financial clarity by morning.

Paid Media Agent

Create Meta campaigns from chat. No Ads Manager.

Tells you what to run based on your strategy and past performance. Creates the campaign, ad set, and ad -- all paused. You review, say launch, it goes live.

Get Early Access

Capabilities

Full campaign management from chat.
Campaign creation -- creates full Meta campaigns via API: campaign, ad set, ad. Decides objective based on your strategy. Proposes targeting from interest search. Sets budget from your CPA target. Everything created paused.
Creative upload -- takes images from Content Studio (1:1 for feed, 9:16 for stories), uploads to Meta's CDN, generates ad copy variations. You pick the combination.
Budget management -- adjusts daily budgets on ad sets and campaigns directly. Proposes reallocation from underperformers to winners. Cap at 30% increase to protect learning phase.
Performance analysis -- classifies every ad: Strong, Scaling, Watch, Underperformer. Flags fatigue signals (frequency above 3, declining CTR). Proposes pauses with reasons.
Pause/resume -- pauses individual ads or entire ad sets instantly. Resumes when ready.
Audience insights -- analyses who is converting, ranks ad sets by CPA, groups by creative angle, recommends new audiences to test.

How it works

From brief to live campaign in 10 minutes.

Connect

Connect Meta Ads (OAuth). Agent auto-discovers your ad account and pixel.

Brief

Tell the agent what you want to promote. It reads your Strategy, checks your pixel and Calendly, and proposes a campaign structure.

Create

Generate creative in Content Studio (1:1 feed + 9:16 story). Attach in chat.

Build

Agent builds the campaign -- targeting, budget, creative, copy -- all paused. Shows you a full preview.

Launch

Say "launch" and the campaign goes live.

Pixel setup

Pixel installation, handled.

The agent handles pixel installation too. It reads your website's layout file, injects the Meta Pixel code via GitHub, and confirms it's firing. For Calendly, it walks you through the one-step setup in Calendly Admin. No code required from you.

SEO Agent

Your SEO agent writes and publishes. You approve.

Connects to Google Search Console and your website's GitHub repo. Audits your site, finds keyword opportunities, writes optimised blog posts, and publishes them live. No freelancer. No waiting.

Capabilities

From keyword data to published page in 12 minutes.
Full SEO audit -- scans every page for title tags, meta descriptions, schema markup, canonical URLs, internal links, and H1 tags. Cross-references with GSC data. Generates a health report saved to your documents.
Quick wins -- finds pages ranking 11-30 with high impressions. These are one title tag or meta description change away from page 1. Proposes the exact edit, publishes on approval.
Blog post writing -- identifies keyword gaps from GSC, writes a fully optimised post (title under 60 chars, meta under 160 chars, structured H2s, internal links, 800-1500 words), generates a hero image in Content Studio, publishes to your site.
Technical SEO -- checks robots.txt, sitemap, broken internal links, missing alt tags, duplicate titles. Opens fixes directly on your website.
Competitor gap analysis -- reads your Strategy and Market & Competitors docs, cross-references with GSC, identifies keywords where competitors outrank you, proposes content or on-page changes to close the gap.

How it works

Five steps to organic growth.

Connect

Connect Google Search Console (OAuth) and GitHub (OAuth). Point the agent at your website repo and blog directory. 5 minutes.

Audit

The SEO agent runs a full audit on first connection. Every page scanned. GSC data pulled. A structured report saved to your Documents tab.

Quick wins

The agent identifies pages where a small edit could move you from page 2 to page 1. It proposes the exact change and publishes on your approval.

Content

The agent proposes blog topics based on keyword gaps. You pick one. It writes the full post, generates the hero image, and publishes it to your site.

Track

GSC data updates weekly. The agent monitors rankings, flags drops, and proposes follow-up actions. You see the impact in Analytics.

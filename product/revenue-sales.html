Capabilities

Your full pipeline, managed daily.
Unified CRM -- leads from Instantly cold outreach, Calendly bookings, and website forms flow into one pipeline. No manual import. No spreadsheets.
Pipeline stages -- Open, Interested, Meeting Booked, Meeting Completed, Won, Not Interested. Every stage tracked automatically from source data.
Lead source tracking -- every lead tagged with its source: Instantly (cold outreach), Calendly (booking), Website (inbound form). Filter and analyse by source.
Contact status -- separate from lead status. Tracks email engagement: Contacted, Opened, Replied, Bounced. Know exactly where every lead stands in both the sales funnel and the communication history.
Outreach drafts -- Revenue agent drafts personalised follow-ups for stale leads, recovery messages for no-shows, conversion outreach for engaged prospects. You review and send.
No-show recovery -- automatic detection of missed meetings from Calendly. Agent drafts recovery messages within 24 hours.
Revenue tracking -- Stripe and Telr payment data alongside the pipeline. See which leads converted to paying customers.

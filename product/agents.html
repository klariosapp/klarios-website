The loop

Agents don't suggest. They do.

Every Klarios agent follows the same loop:

Read

Pulls data from your connected tools (Meta Ads, GSC, Xero, Stripe, CRM).

Analyse

Compares against your strategy document and past performance.

Propose

Presents a numbered plan in chat with specific actions.

Execute

On your approval, calls the API directly: publishes the post, creates the campaign, drafts the email.

Report

Confirms the action is live and saves a record to your documents.

You are always in the loop. Nothing goes live without your approval. But you never have to log into Ads Manager, GitHub, or Xero to make it happen.
